Methods of Password Security



1. Password Managers




Some services keep your password, if it's complex or random. Google for example offers a password keeper, Personally this is one of the most easiest to keep. The only thing you need to watch out is to Remember the Google Account Password itself.

2. Personal Stuff in Passwords




Since we want to make sure no one guesses our password, You can try adding your Pet's Name, Favorite Food, Favorite Number, etc. to your Password, It will be hard to guess for Hackers



3. Symbols in Password




When you are making a password, you can also add some symbols to it that are accessible to the keyboard. don't use any unicode that can't be typed on a regular keyboard or it can be difficult to get to that point. add a few symbols like "@" or "!", etc.
